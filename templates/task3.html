What is Jaccard Similarity?

It measures the overlap between two sets (genres). In this chart, it represents how likely a user who likes Genre A is to also like Genre B.

  • 1.0: Perfect overlap (Identical audiences)
  • 0.0: No overlap (Mutually exclusive)
